Bean cake (Akara)

Bean cake, is a very popular West African snack prepared by frying a paste made from ground beans

Bean cake is prepared by blending and mixing peeled brown beans, scotch bonnent peper,onion and salt, and then fried unitll golden brown. It's a good source of protein, fiber, and iron. it can be a healthy and convenient option for those seeking vegan or vegetarian snack. Is can be served with bread, pap and custard

Ingredients

  • beans
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • onions
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
